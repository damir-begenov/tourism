A Journey Across Boundless Landscapes

Discover the Majesty of Vast Territory

The grand map of Kazakhstan unveils a captivating tapestry of landscapes and cultures, inviting travelers on a remarkable journey across this expansive nation. With its boundless beauty and diverse traditions, Kazakhstan offers a unique blend of natural wonders, historical treasures, and warm hospitality that will leave a lasting impression.

Tour de Astana

  • Nur-Alem
    Future energy museum

    The symbol of EXPO-2017 will be the pavilion of Kazakhstan in the form of the biggest complete sphere in the world. In addition to the Museum of the Future, The Sphere will accommodate a museum telling the history of the country and the history of Kazakhstan nations. To-date, foundation for this building has been completed.

  • Abu Dhabi Plaza
    Multifunctional complex

    Central business district, Nur-Sultan, Abu Dhabi Plaza is a mixed-use mall, office and residential tower in the heart of Nur-Sultan, the capital of Kazakhstan, opened in December 2019. The development’s tallest tower houses residential and office premises over 75 floors, supported by two further A-class office towers and a 15-floor residential tower. A 4-star Sheraton Hotel offers 190 rooms and 100 serviced apartments. There’s also car parking for more than 4,000 mall visitors, apartment residents and office employees.

    Location:

    st. Siganak, 60/5
